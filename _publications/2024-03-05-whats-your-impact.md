Research is essential to ensure good quality palliative care, yet important research can be wasted when it is not communicated and implemented. In an ongoing study, Tobias Constien highlights a way to mobilise research for impact and invites researchers to connect with the research project to help create resources to maximise the reach of palliative care research.
